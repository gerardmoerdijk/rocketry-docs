# Applied Rocketry Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation and safety repository for Applied Rocketry.

This platform serves as the central resource for safe, legally compliant model rocketry in South Africa. Our guidelines are adapted from the National Association of Rocketry (NAR) global standards, strictly aligned with the South African Civil Aviation Authority (SACAA) airspace regulations and the Explosives Act (Act 15 of 2003).
