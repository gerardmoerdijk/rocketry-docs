# South African Model Rocket Safety Code

This code outlines the mandatory safety protocols for operating Class 1.4G model rockets in South Africa. It is an adaptation of the NAR Model Rocket Safety Code, localized for strict compliance with South African law.

## 1. Materials and Limits

* **Materials:** I will use only lightweight, non-metal parts for the nose, body, and fins of my rocket.
* **Weight Limits:** My rocket will weigh no more than **1.5 kg** at liftoff.
* **Propellant Limits:** My rocket will contain no more than **125 g** of propellant.
* *Note: Exceeding these limits requires explicit, prior clearance from the South African Civil Aviation Authority (SACAA).*

## 2. Motors and Ignition

* **Motors:** I will use only certified, commercially made model rocket motors classified as 1.4G under the South African Explosives Act (Act 15 of 2003). I will not tamper with these motors or use them for any purpose except those recommended by the manufacturer.
* **Ignition System:** I will launch my rockets with an electrical launch system and electrical motor igniters. My system will include a safety interlock in series with the launch switch, and will use a launch switch that returns to the "off" position when released.
* **Misfires:** If my rocket does not launch when I press the button, I will remove the launcher's safety interlock or disconnect its battery, and will wait **60 seconds** after the last launch attempt before allowing anyone to approach the rocket.

## 3. Launch Operations

* **Launch Safety:** I will use a countdown before launch, and will ensure that everyone is paying attention and is at a safe distance.
* **Safe Distances:** I will stand at least **5 meters** away from the launch pad for motors up to class D, and **10 meters** away for larger motors.
* **Launch Angle:** I will launch my rocket outdoors, in clear weather, with wind speeds no greater than **32 km/h**. I will ensure the launch rod or tower is pointed within 20 degrees of vertical.

## 4. Environmental Safety

* **Fire Mitigation:** Due to the high risk of veld fires, I will ensure a cleared area of at least a **1.5-meter radius** (bare soil, concrete, or cleared dirt) around the launch pad.
* **Recovery:** I will use a flame-resistant or fireproof recovery system wadding in my rocket to prevent fires from the motor's ejection charge.
* **Flight Path:** I will not launch my rocket at targets, into clouds, or near airplanes, and will never put any flammable or explosive payload in my rocket.
